Spaghetto

Description

The spaghetto is a beautiful cheap dish composed of spaghetti ,caramelized onions, grilled tomatoes and sausage.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Sausage
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Heat water to a rolling boil
  2. Cut up onions, tomato and sausages into small squares
  3. Cook these three in olive oil
  4. Add spaghetti to boiling water and leave for 7 min
  5. Remove and rinse spaghetti and put into serving bowl
  6. Add warm cooked ingredients
  7. Add pesto and ricotta cheese
  8. Mix and serve!
